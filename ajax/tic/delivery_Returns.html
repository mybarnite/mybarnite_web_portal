Delivery & Returns

 

How does it work?

Before you buy a ticket for a prize draw be sure to check delivery information. You can see here if the item is to be delivered or to be collected in person.

Some Sellers will also have specific postage restrictions and may not post oversees for example. 

 

For sellers

Be sure to note any special postage restrictions or requirements when creating your prize draw. Also note that you must include any postage costs in ticket price! 

 

Returns

We hope you love your new purchase. However, if you change your mind, some sellers accept returns.

Check out the details of a seller’s returns policy on the item listing page for the draw. This will also tell you who is responsible for paying returns postage costs.

If you receive an item that’s damaged or wasn’t as described in the listing, you’re covered for the purchase price and original postage under the PayPal's Money Back Guarantee  because we exclusively use PayPal.
