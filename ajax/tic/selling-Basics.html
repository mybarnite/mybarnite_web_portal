Selling Basics

Here's how to get started:

 

1.

Create an Account

Before you start selling, you need to create a Tickhit account.

Be sure to check that all contact information is correct so that the winner can contact you to arrange collection or delivery of the item. Creating an account is simple and only takes a minute. For best results we recommend that you upload a profile picture to make your self distinctive 

 

2.

Create Your Prize Draw

We have worked hard to make sure that creating a prize draw listing is very quick and easy.

To create a listing simply click "sell" link at the top left of the website (next to "my account") or you can create a listing directly from your "my account" page by clicking on "My Lstings" then click the "Create Listing" button. 

*Check if the Prize Item that you are offering is allowed to be listed on Tickhit.com. If you are unsure then you can check our list of all prohibited items to see what is allowed to be offered as prizes* 

Next you follow the simple 5 step system;

1)     Choose a title for your listing (this will be the title that everyone sees)

 

2)     Choose a Category; Simply choose a category that best fits your item (this will be where your item will found on Tickhit.com).

3)     Upload some pictures of your item(s) (you can upload between up to 6 pictures). We recommend uploading as many pictures as possible, drawing attention to any flaws or defects so that users know what they are getting.

4)     Describe what you are selling; Write a description of your prize draw - let buyers know what you have for sale and be as complete as possible.

 

5)     Set a price (individual ticket price will be automatically calculated). We recommend that you spend some time and do research on the item to understand the correct price *REMEMBER THAT THERE ARE A TOTAL OF 39 TICKETS THAT MUST BE SOLD BEFORE THE WINNER IS DRAWN*

Here you also specify if you will post (deliver) the item or if buyer must collect. You can also specify any special postage instructions (for example if you are willing to post internationally or locally).

Once your listing has been created you can find it in "My Listings" Tab inside of your "My Account" page.

You can edit various elements of your listing at any point such as your title or description.

 

LISTING FEES:
All listing fees are calculated as 5% of total prize cost (for example if you list item for £100 total then listing fee will be £5.00)

 No fees are paid until all prize draw tickets for your listing have been sold so you never pay to list until your item has sold!

 

3.

Sell Tickets

Once your prize draw listing is active you must sell all 39 tickets within 28 days.

Ticket sales is the easiest part for the seller - you will receive notifications each time someone purchases a ticket for your draw and another notification once all tickets have been sold.

 

**NOTE THAT YOU CAN SHARE ANY OR ALL OF YOUR LISTINGS TO ALL POPULAR SOCIAL NETWORKS JUST BY CLICKING SHARE BUTTONS**

 

Once all 39 tickets have been sold your item will be "ready to draw" and you will receive a notification that all tickets have been sold.

 

HOW TO CHOOSE A WINNER:

You simply log into your account, and click the button on the listing to set status to “Ready to Draw”. Tickhit will then automatically check the next available irish lottery bonus ball number and identify a winner.

 

When a winner is selected you will be notified that your draw has completed and a winner has been selected. At this point you will be able to contact the winner to arrange delivery or collection of the prize.

4.

Draw Results

Once all 39 tickets have been sold and you have set listing has been set to "Ready to Draw" we will automatically check the next Irish Lottery and identify a winner. We will look specifically for the "Bonus Ball" result and match this against the winning ticket.

 

We will automatically contact both the prize seller and the winning ticket holder informing each that this draw has completed and allowing for exchange of contact details to arrange for collection or delivery of the prize.

 

Simply contact the winner and arrange for delivery or collection of the prize!

 

5.

Feedback

Tickhit.com has a feedback system so that we can build a trusted community of great prize draw players and creators.

Don’t forget to leave valuable feedback for the buyer to make Tickhit a better place!
